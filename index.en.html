DINING

One restaurant. Two kitchens.
Dine anywhere on 10,000 sqm.

Zatiny is the heart of Matlai — one restaurant housing two kitchens under the same roof. But the magic of a small boutique is that you are not tied to a single table. Your butler will set up dining wherever you wish on the property — balcony at sunrise, garden after dark, beach with your feet in the sand.

ZATINY KITCHEN · BY MATLAI

Zatiny — fine dining on the beach.

Oceanfront atmosphere, fresh seafood, Mediterranean and Swahili-leaning plates, a quiet wine list. Cocktails and mocktails from the bar. The restaurant opens from 10:00; the bar serves breakfast at any time, no problem.

Romantic dinners · Special occasions · Honeymoon experiences
DUYÊN KITCHEN · BY KIM

Duyên — home cooking from the heart.

The second kitchen inside Zatiny — Vietnamese-Zanzibari fusion home cooking by Kim. Bao buns, shaking beef, fragrant herbs from the garden. The kind of food made with care, eaten slowly, the way home cooking is meant to be.

Same restaurant, different kitchen · Family-style plates · Beachfront setting

BUSTANI SPA

Bustani Spa.
Tropical garden. Skilled hands.

Bustani means garden in Kiswahili. The spa lives inside the Matlai garden — a quiet pavilion under the palms, where treatments combine Oriental, Asian, and European techniques with spices, oils and herbs from Zanzibar.

Led by Maryam Salim Hassan — trained at the Ahlan Palace Institute in Stone Town, with Matlai since 2014.

QUIET QUESTIONS

The things guests usually ask first.

If anything is missing here, the easiest answer is WhatsApp — we reply within a few hours, often within minutes during business hours in Zanzibar (UTC+3).

Can I book a single room, or do I need to book the whole villa?
Both. You can book a single room or suite, or take a whole villa for exclusive use. Villa Kidosho holds up to 4 guests; Asili House up to 8 in double-room configuration. We add extra beds for families and bigger groups on request.
How do I get to Matlai from the airport?
Zanzibar International Airport (ZNZ) is about 70 minutes by road. On request, we arrange the transfer through a trusted driver company — just send us your flight details. Payment is made on arrival at the hotel by credit card machine or cash.
Is Matlai suitable for families with children?
Yes. Asili House is particularly well suited for families — four bedrooms, large oceanfront pool, garden, butler service, extra beds available for kids on request. We have welcomed many families since 2012 and the team is genuinely good with children.
What's included in Half Board?
Breakfast served on your private balcony (or the restaurant if you prefer — just tell your butler the evening before), plus dinner at Zatiny or Duyên. Lunch and drinks are à la carte. Full Board upgrade available.
When is the best time to visit?
June–October is the cooler, drier high season — clearest waters and the most reliable weather. December–February is hot and busy (festive). March–May has lower rates and quieter beaches, with brief warm rains. We are happy to advise on dates by WhatsApp.
How do I confirm a booking?
Send a non-binding enquiry through the booking page or WhatsApp us with your preferred dates and number of guests. We reply with live availability, your best rate, and a hold for 48 hours while you decide.
